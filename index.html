For Beginners/Starters

April 9th, 2016

       Everyone wants to be slim and sexy, but not many want to be in the gym getting down on some squats and sweating on some random elliptical. I do not know if the motivation is missing for most people, or if people just do not know what to do. I know that motivation can be found everywhere, but in all honesty motivation has to be something you install and drill into your head so that you don’t forget why you started.

Here are my five best advices I give to all startners--

       #1. Ask yourself these couple of questions. Why are you doing what you are doing? Are you doing it for others, or yourself? Is this something you are willing to put in all your effort and not half-ass it? Can you even make time for such a change? Take your time and think this through before you make your decision.

|Knowing the answer to these questions will allow you to know exactly why you are changing your lifestyle. Knowing the "why" for everything you do can be your biggest motivation.|

       #2. MAKE GOALS ! Get yourself a journal and write down all of your short term and long term goals. After you write down all your goals, make a plan. You can never go wrong with making plans for all your goals.

|This way, you are taking action into achieving your goals and not just writing them down.|

       #3. RESEARCH! RESEARCH! REASEARCH! I say this all the time to people that come up to me asking questions about what they should do.My answer is always Do Your RESEARCH ! DO YOUR RESEARCH !

|What people fail to understand is that not everything works for everyone. Because we are all different, some things might works for us and not others. Just because one person did something and they saw results does not mean you will see results too. If living a healthy lifestyle is what you want than you should be willing to do your research so that you are knowledgeable about what you are doing.|

       #4. Create a fitness motivational board, or make a fitness Instagram, tumblr, Pintrist; whatever it is that you will look at everyday.

|Motivation is key when changing your lifestyle because there are days when you will feel unmotivated and feel like you are not making any progress. So by having something in front of you that reminds you of your goals, you will reach them goals sooner than you thought. Make sure you always keep your motivatonal boards updated. |

      #5. START ! START ! START !

(You do not need a gym membership to start, you can workout right at home. All you need is a screen and internet! That’s it ! Now start !)
